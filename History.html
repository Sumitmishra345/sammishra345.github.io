Our Journey Through Time

Since our founding in 2023, RYDR has grown from a small pilot in Mumbai to a nationwide service.

  1. 2023: Launch in Mumbai with 100 riders. First 10,000 packages delivered.
  2. 2024: Expanded to Delhi/NCR and Bengaluru. Introduced real-time tracking.
  3. 2025: Launched intercity “By End of Day” service and truck deliveries.
  4. 2026: Partnered with major brands: Amazon, Maruti Suzuki, Barbeque Nation.

We continue innovating—soon across 15+ cities with our RYDR One platform.
