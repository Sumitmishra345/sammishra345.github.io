Privacy Policy

RYDR Delivery (“we”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information.

1. Information Collection

  • Personal Data: Name, address, phone number, email.
  • Order Details: Pickup & drop locations, weight, parcel value.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device info, browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill delivery orders.
  • To communicate status updates and support requests.
  • To improve and personalize our services.
  • For fraud detection and security monitoring.

3. Data Sharing

  • With delivery partners to complete your orders.
  • With service providers (e.g., payment gateways).
  • As required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Security Measures

We implement encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information anytime.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

Effective date: 01 July 2025
